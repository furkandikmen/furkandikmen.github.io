Research interests

My research focuses on natural language ontology and compositional semantics, with a particular specialization in object-based truthmaker semantics. I also work on morphosyntactic questions, especially insofar as they relate to semantic interpretation, including in frameworks such as Distributed Morphology and Nanosyntax. My empirical domains include clausal complementation, argument and event structure, passives, discourse particles, case, and diminutives.
