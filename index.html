Hello!

I am Furkan Dikmen. I am a PhD candidate in Language Sciences at Université Côte d'Azur, in France. I work as a doctoral candidate in CNRS, BCL, as part of the project IMMAGES, under the supervision of Friederike Moltmann. I am primarily interested in issues in semantics and pragmatics as well as morphosyntax. You can learn more about my previous and ongoing research at Publications tab. 

 

My research interests:

Semantics of plurality, argument/event structure, syntax/semantics of passive clauses, pragmatics, discourse structure/particles, nano-syntax, result state modification, compositionality, compounding and nominalization, complementation, semantics of diminutives/degree semantics
